My sandbox

In my job I had the opportunity to work with various content management systems such as Wordpress, Contao or Drupal as editor. However, in my spare time I am often trying new web frameworks and tools (such as GatsbyyJS, Wordpress or Jekyll) as a developer and designer for the sake of learning, and mostly because I enjoy coding. I also successfully completed the almost 30-hour long LinkedIn Learning Path "Junior Wordpress Developer", where I learned the basics of Wordpress as a developer. A particular interest of mine when developing is clean code and accessibility - a topic I already wrote a number of papers about at university.
