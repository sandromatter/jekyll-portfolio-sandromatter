My sandbox

In my work life I had the opportunity to use various content management systems such as Wordpress, Contao or Drupal as editor. However, in my spare time I often experiment with new web frameworks and tools (such as GatsbyJS, Wordpress or Jekyll) as a developer and designer for the sake of learning, and mostly because I enjoy coding. I also successfully completed the almost 30-hour LinkedIn Learning Path "Junior Wordpress Developer", where I learned the basics of Wordpress from a developer point of view. A particular interest of mine during development, is clean code and accessibility - a topic I have written a number of papers about, in my studies.
