My daily business

I have been working in marketing agencies for more than three years. At first, I was working as a desktop publisher, often doing creative work, such as layouting, designing and photography, until I started working increasingly in the digital field. At the moment I'm working part-time as an online marketing specialist besides studying for my bachelors degree, which I will finish next summer. I switched my job because the digital world was and still is exciting to me. I am very familiar with tools like Google Ads, Facebook Business and Ads Manager or Google Analytics and the Tag Manager - but still try to improve constantly. After all, there is something new to learn every day. In my daily business I work in the small agency "Stutz Kommunikation" where we assist SMEs to promote their businesses, guide them through the digital jungle and help them to win new customers.
