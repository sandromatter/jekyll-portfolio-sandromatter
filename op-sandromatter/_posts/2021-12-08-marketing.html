My daily business

I have been working in marketing agencies for more than four years. At first, I was working as a desktop publisher, often doing creative work, such as layouting, designing and photography, until I started working increasingly in the digital field. Currently I am working part-time as an online marketing specialist besides studying for my bachelors degree, which I will finish next summer. Tools like Google Ads, Facebook Business and Ads Manager or Google Analytics and the Tag Manager are all part of my daily work in the agency "Stutz Kommunikation". There we assist SMEs to promote their businesses, guide them through the digital jungle and help them to win new customers.
