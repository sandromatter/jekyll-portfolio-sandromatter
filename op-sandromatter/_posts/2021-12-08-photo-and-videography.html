Photography on the job …

Photo- and videography: Topics that I was always interested about. I learnt the basics of photography in my apprenticeship and used the knowledge later in my career. I had the chance of taking photos of clients and for the newspaper "Zofinger Tagblatt". My photos got printed and published several times. During my time at the digital agency #ztplus, organising photo shoots was also part of my job. Besides taking photos behind the photo camera, I was regularly behind the video camera for live tv broadcasts in the studio of the local media outlet.

… and my personal projects

My camera is always with me, especially when travelling. I love exploring cities with my camera and taking unique photos. My Fuji X100s from 2013 is still considered my best buy ever. The little cam has been everywhere with me: in Australia, New York, Paris and of course in the Swiss mountains. I cannot wait to take it to Canada!
