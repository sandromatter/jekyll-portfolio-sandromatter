About me

I am a multi-talented person and have been working in various marketing agencies for almost five years. Besides my job, I love riding my mountain bike – my other major passion next to marketing and the web.

I have been employed in marketing agencies as a desktop publisher and online marketing specialist and also had the opportunity to gain experience as media designer, photographer and camera operator. My professional career started 9 years ago, when I began my Swiss apprenticeship as designer of digital and print media.

Ever since I can remember, I have always been curious. In my opinion, constantly learning and experimenting is part of life just as much as it is part of business. I believe there is something new and exciting to discover and learn every day. I love to keep learning, continue to challenge myself and do interesting things that matter to me. My motto is: Try. Fail. Improve. Because making mistakes, improving constantly, and adapting is how you learn best. As a person, I am calm and stay focused even in stressful situations.

My background

After my compulsory education, I completed an apprenticeship as a designer of digital and print media at ZT Medien AG, working with tools such as the Adobe suite in the prepress department. Subsequently, I switched from the print department to the digital agency of the media company, called #ztplus. In these two years, I planned and realized online marketing campaigns, assisted in film productions, implemented web projects, organized photo shootings and consulted for the introduction of a new editorial system. In 2019, I started a part-time bachelor programme “Digital Business Management” at the University of Applied Sciences of the Grisons, which I graduated from in summer 2022. Ever since, I started working at swisspeace Swiss Peace Foundation in the communications team and freelance as an Online Marketing Specialist at the advertising agencies Stutz Kommunikation and ADi AG.
